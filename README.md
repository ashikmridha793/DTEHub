## 🔍 What is DTEHub?

DTEHub collects resources for all branches and syllabus schemes such as C-19, C-20, and C-25. It contains notes, question papers, and specially designed tools like a DCET Rank Predictor. The predictor calculates your rank based on your CGPA and DCET marks. You also get a workspace where you can save your favorite files and track your recent activity.

## 📋 Features at a Glance

- **Resource Access:** Find notes and papers by branch and syllabus.
- **DCET Rank Predictor:** Use your scores to estimate your DCET rank.
- **Personal Workspace:** Save favorites, downloads, and view history.
- **Preferences Saved:** Your branch and syllabus choices stay set automatically.
- **Simple Interface:** Designed for easy browsing and searching.

## 🎯 Using DTEHub

### Browsing Resources

- Use the search bar at the top to enter a subject, topic, or paper year.
- Filter results by branch or syllabus using the options on the left.
- Click on any resource to open or download it.

### DCET Rank Predictor

- Go to the **Rank Predictor** tab.
- Enter your Diploma CGPA and DCET marks in the boxes.
- Click **Calculate** to see your predicted rank based on the app’s algorithm.

### Smart Workspace

- Use the **Workspace** tab to see files you have marked as favorite.
- Access your recent downloads and viewed items here.
- Workspace data saves locally so your preferences stay between sessions.
